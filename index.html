AI Integration & Automation

AI integration means adding smart tools into your existing apps, websites, or business processes so they can think, learn, and automate tasks. Instead of doing everything manually, AI helps handle things like customer support, data analysis, content creation, and decision-making. For non-tech users, think of it like this: you connect tools such as ChatGPT or Zapier to your daily work so tasks run automatically in the background. This saves time, reduces effort, and makes your work faster and more efficient without needing coding skills.
